About Me

Assistant Professor
Department of Earth Sciences
University of Oregon

I am a seismologist broadly interested in tectonic controls on seismic hazard. I investigate the physical mechanisms controlling earthquake nucleation, rupture, and arrest across a variety of depths, from shallow earthquakes to Earth’s deepest tremors. I explore Earth's interior across a variety of scales, from shallow crustal heterogeneities that impact hazard estimates to subduction zone systems that inform our understanding of plate tectonics.

I'm actively recruiting students and post-docs to join my research group. Please reach out if you're interested in exploring our planet with seismology.
